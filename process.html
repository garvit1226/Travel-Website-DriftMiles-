Process for booking a trip

  1. Visit the Website: Open your travel website on a browser or mobile app.
  2. Sign Up / Log In: Create an account or log in to access personalized features and save trip details.
  3. Search for Destinations: Enter the destination, travel dates, and number of travelers in the search bar.
  4. Explore Options: Browse through flights, hotels, packages, or tours available for the selected destination.
  5. Select a Package: Click on the preferred option to see detailed information, pricing, and itinerary.
  6. Customize Trip (Optional): Choose add-ons like extra nights, meals, sightseeing tours, or special activities.
  7. Traveler Details: Fill in personal information for each traveler, including names, contact info, and any special requirements.
  8. Review Booking: Check all details such as dates, pricing, inclusions, and terms before confirming.
  9. Make Payment: Choose a payment method (credit/debit card, net banking, or wallet) and complete the payment securely.
  10. Confirmation: Get an instant booking confirmation via email or SMS with all trip details and e-tickets.
  11. Booking: Users can log in anytime to view, modify, or cancel their bookings if needed.

Important Documents You Should Carry While Traveling

Passport-
Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.
Visa-
Depending on your destination country's entry requirements.
Flight Tickets / E-tickets-
For both onward and return journeys.
Travel Insurance-
Covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, or lost luggage.
Financial Proofs-
Credit/debit cards, forex/travel card, and some local currency cash.
Vaccination Certificates-
Required in some countries (e.g., Yellow Fever, COVID-19).
